<h1>Use QR codes to share files</h1>


Create your own page on the internet with logo, text and files. Upload your menu, photos, music or instruction manuals. Monitor how often it is scanned



<p>This is useful when you want to share some information or files with your clients. The use cases are unlimited, they can include:</p>

<ul>
    <li>Menus for a restaurant or a cafe</li>
    <li>Instructions manuals for appliances in the hotel or Airbnb</li>
    <li>Additional information for products or exhibition items</li>
    <li>Photo albums, music samples and many more</li>
</ul>
